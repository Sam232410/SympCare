About Sympcare

SympCare is an intelligent diagnostic system that leverages OCR, NLP, and Machine Learning to analyze Complete Blood Count reports. It automatically extracts hematological parameters, standardizes data through alias mapping and unit scaling, and predicts potential medical conditions.

Our Mission

To develop an intelligent, accessible, and secure system that extracts, interprets, and analyzes CBC data through OCR and Machine Learning.

Our Vision

To revolutionize healthcare diagnostics by enabling accurate, data-driven disease prediction using automated analysis of medical reports.
